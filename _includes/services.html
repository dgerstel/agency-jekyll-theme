How do we work?

First, in a free chat , we ask you many questions to understand your current situation (problem) and where you would like to be (solution). This allows us to come up with a proposal to pave the way to accomplish your objectives the most efficiently.



Then, we proceed with the execution. Once the first goal is defined, we come up with a prototype (usually a few day progress) and ask you for feedback. This allows to re-adjust the definition and repeat the prototype-feedback loop. Finally, you receive the product "with batteries", so you can use it autonomously.
